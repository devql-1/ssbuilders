Founder & CEO

Seth Sanwariya

"Building more than properties — we build trust, relationships, and legacies that last generations."

With over two decades of experience in Rajasthan's real estate landscape, Seth Sanwariya founded SS Builders with a singular vision: to create spaces that blend modern luxury with traditional values. Under his leadership, SS Builders has delivered over 500 premium residential and commercial projects across Jaipur and surrounding areas.

His deep understanding of local market dynamics, combined with an unwavering commitment to quality and transparency, has earned SS Builders the trust of thousands of families. Every project bears his personal touch — ensuring timely delivery, premium materials, and designs that stand the test of time.
